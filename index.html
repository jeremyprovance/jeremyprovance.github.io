Hi , I'm Jeremy!

I'm an interdisciplinary PhD student at the Univeristy of Missouri-Kansas City School of Medicine studying health informatics. My research at the Saint Luke's Mid American Heart Institute is under the mentorship of Kim Smolderen, PhD. We aim to use health data to better understand chronic disease, disease outcomes, and to empower patients to take an active role in creating better health.
